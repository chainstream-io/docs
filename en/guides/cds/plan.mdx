### Subscription Changes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upgrading Subscription">
    Select the desired plan (Premium, Business, or Enterprise) in your account settings and confirm. Upgrades apply immediately, and your payment method is charged instantly.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Downgrading Subscription">
    Choose a lower-tier plan within your account settings. Changes take immediate effect. Your billing is adjusted instantly based on the new plan.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Reactivating Subscription">
    Reactivate by choosing your desired subscription in your account settings. Reactivation occurs immediately, and billing resumes instantly.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Cancelling Subscription">
    To cancel your subscription, select "Cancel Subscription" in your account settings. Cancellation is effective at the end of your current billing period.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Payment & Refund Policy

<Note>
Please note that all subscription changes are final, and no refunds are provided. Any remaining API credits will not be transferred to your new plan.
</Note>
