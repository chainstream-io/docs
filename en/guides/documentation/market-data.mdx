## Liquidity & Order Book Data

<Card>
  Liquidity represents the ease with which an asset can be traded without causing significant price movements. Chainstream's Advanced Liquidity Analytics aggregates real-time orderbook data across multiple exchanges, providing two critical indicators—Liquidity Delta and Spread Distribution—to deliver deep insights into market sentiment, buying/selling pressure, and volatility.
</Card>

### Key Features

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Aggregated Liquidity Data" icon="chart-network">
    <div className="space-y-4">
      <div>
        <strong>Definition:</strong> Chainstream consolidates liquidity data from various DEX and CEX platforms, providing unified market-depth visibility.
      </div>
      <div>
        <strong>Purpose:</strong> Aggregated data eliminates single-exchange anomalies, offering an accurate representation of the overall market liquidity.
      </div>
      <div>
        <strong>Visualization:</strong> Unified metrics across all liquidity indicators and orderbook visualizations, ensuring consistency and clarity.
      </div>
    </div>
  </Card>

  <Card title="Orderbook Liquidity Delta" icon="wave-sine">
    <div className="space-y-4">
      <div>
        <strong>Definition:</strong> Liquidity Delta measures the net difference between bid (buy) and ask (sell) volumes within a ±1% range around the current market price.
      </div>
      <div>
        <strong>Purpose:</strong> Reveals immediate market sentiment, highlighting whether buying (positive delta) or selling (negative delta) is dominant.
      </div>
      <div>
        <strong>Visualization:</strong> Real-time chart illustrating shifts in aggregated liquidity across exchanges, dynamically updating market sentiment.
      </div>
    </div>
  </Card>

  <Card title="Orderbook Spread Distribution" icon="arrows-left-right">
    <div className="space-y-4">
      <div>
        <strong>Definition:</strong> Illustrates the range and frequency of bid-ask spreads aggregated over selected intervals (e.g., 1-minute, 5-minute, 15-minute).
      </div>
      <div>
        <strong>Purpose:</strong> Enables users to assess market liquidity consistency and potential slippage when executing orders.
      </div>
      <div>
        <strong>Visualization:</strong> Interactive charts displaying spread fluctuations and their mean values over customizable timeframes.
      </div>
    </div>
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Factor

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Understanding Liquidity Delta" icon="magnifying-glass-chart">
    <div className="space-y-4">
      <div>
        The Orderbook Liquidity Delta chart provides critical insights into market sentiment:
        <div className="mt-2">
          <strong>Positive Delta:</strong> Suggests stronger buying interest with bid volumes exceeding ask volumes. Potentially indicates upward price momentum.
        </div>
        <div className="mt-2">
          <strong>Negative Delta:</strong> Indicates dominant selling activity, with higher ask volumes than bid volumes, suggesting potential downward price pressure.
        </div>
      </div>
      
      <div className="mt-4">
        <strong>Important Note:</strong>
        <div className="mt-2">
          Liquidity Delta offers immediate market sentiment insight but does not directly translate to guaranteed price movements. Factors, like hidden "iceberg" orders and large market orders, can significantly affect actual trading outcomes.
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </Card>

  <Card title="Analyzing Spread Distribution" icon="chart-line">
    <div className="space-y-4">
      <div>
        The Spread Distribution chart helps traders evaluate market liquidity dynamics and transaction cost implications:
        <div className="mt-2">
          <strong>Narrow Spread:</strong> Implies high liquidity, efficient execution, and minimal price impact.
        </div>
        <div className="mt-2">
          <strong>Wide Spread:</strong> Indicates lower liquidity, increased execution costs, and higher risk of price slippage.
        </div>
      </div>
      
      <div className="mt-4">
        Analyzing spread distribution over varying intervals helps identify optimal trade execution times and potential market volatility risks.
      </div>
    </div>
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Important Considerations

<Card title="Limitations and Best Practices" icon="triangle-exclamation">
  <div className="space-y-4">

    <div className="mt-4">
      <strong>Real-Time Snapshot:</strong>

    <div className="mt-4">
