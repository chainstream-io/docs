## Privacy Policy

<Info>
Chainstream ("we," "our," or "us") provides a real-time blockchain data streaming platform for developers, analysts, and institutions. This Privacy Policy is intended to help you understand how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information and how you can exercise your privacy rights.
</Info>

### Scope

<Note>
This Privacy Policy applies to personal information we collect through our website, documentation portal, APIs, terminals, SDKs, and any associated services (collectively referred to as the "Services").
</Note>

### Changes to this Privacy Policy

<Note>
We may revise this Privacy Policy at any time. If material changes are made, we will notify you via email or by updating the policy date. Your continued use of our Services constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms.
</Note>

### Information We Collect

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Information You Provide to Us" icon="user">
    - Account Registration: When you register for a Chainstream account, we collect your name, email, organization name (optional), password, and blockchain wallet addresses (where applicable).
    - API Usage: When requesting API keys or accessing paid features, we may collect billing information and payment details.
    - Support and Communications: If you contact us for support or feedback, we collect your communications and any additional data you provide.
    - Token Submissions: If you submit token updates or use services like our Token Info Update Service, we may collect metadata, token images, social links, and related information.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Information We Collect Automatically" icon="robot">
    - Log Data: Includes IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, device identifiers, timestamps, API call metrics, and user agent.
    - Usage Analytics: We automatically track API usage (including topic subscriptions, query volumes, and response times) to improve performance and ensure fair usage.
    - Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, analyze usage, and personalize content. These include essential, analytics, and preference cookies.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Information from Third Parties" icon="link">
    - Integrations: When you integrate your Chainstream account with third-party tools (e.g., wallets, exchanges), we may receive related data as permitted by those services.
    - Publicly Available Blockchain Data: We index and associate blockchain data that is already publicly accessible, including wallet addresses and transaction histories.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Card title="Data Usage Purposes" icon="chart-mixed">
We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve our Services

- Respond to user inquiries and provide technical support
- Send service-related notices and communications
- Process billing and subscription-related tasks
- Conduct internal analytics and develop new features
- Enforce our Terms of Service and prevent fraud
</Card>
