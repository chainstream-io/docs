## Chainstream Account

Welcome to your Chainstream Account setup guide. This section will help you effortlessly register, activate, and utilize your Chainstream account, enabling quick access to Chainstream Terminal and robust data APIs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register a Chainstream Account" icon="user-plus">
    If you don't already have a Chainstream account, simply navigate to our registration page and fill out the required details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Activate Your Account" icon="envelope-circle-check">
    Upon registering, you'll receive an activation email containing a link to confirm your account. Please activate your account via the provided link to access advanced features and generate API keys.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Logging In" icon="right-to-bracket">
    After successful activation, log into your Chainstream account using your email and password or other log-in methods for enhanced security and convenience.
  </Step>
</Steps>
